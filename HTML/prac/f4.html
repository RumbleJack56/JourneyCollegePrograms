Courses at Delhi Technological University

Delhi Technological University (DTU), formerly known as Delhi College of Engineering (DCE), is a renowned institution in India that offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral courses in various fields of engineering, technology, and management. Here's a general description of the courses typically offered by DTU:
  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech):

    • - DTU offers a comprehensive B.Tech program with specializations in areas such as Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, and many more. These programs provide students with a strong foundation in engineering principles and practical skills.

  • Master of Technology (M.Tech):

    • - The university offers M.Tech programs with specializations in various engineering disciplines. These postgraduate programs are designed to provide in-depth knowledge and research opportunities for students who want to specialize in a specific field of engineering.

  • Master of Business Administration (MBA):

    • - DTU also offers an MBA program for students interested in pursuing management education. This program focuses on developing business and leadership skills, preparing graduates for managerial roles in various industries.

  • Master of Science (M.Sc):

    • - In addition to engineering and management programs, DTU offers M.Sc programs in areas such as Mathematics and Computing, Physics, and more. These programs cater to students interested in pursuing higher studies in science-related fields.

  • Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.):

    • - The university provides opportunities for research and doctoral studies in various domains. Students can pursue a Ph.D. in engineering, sciences, and management, among other fields.

  • Interdisciplinary and Dual Degree Programs:

    • - DTU also offers unique interdisciplinary and dual degree programs that allow students to combine engineering with other disciplines or gain multiple qualifications during their academic journey.

  • Continuing Education and Short-Term Courses:

    • - Additionally, DTU may offer continuing education programs and short-term courses for professionals and individuals looking to enhance their skills and knowledge in specific areas.
Delhi Technological University (DTU) has a rich and storied history that dates back to the early 20th century. The institution has undergone several name changes and transformations over the years. Here's a brief overview of the history of Delhi Technological University:
  • Establishment as Delhi Polytechnic (1940): The roots of DTU can be traced back to 1940 when it was established as Delhi Polytechnic. The institution was founded to provide technical education and training to aspiring engineers and technologists in British India.
  • Renamed as Delhi College of Engineering (DCE) (1965): In 1965, Delhi Polytechnic was upgraded and renamed as Delhi College of Engineering (DCE). The institution continued to offer undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology.
  • Affiliation with the University of Delhi (2009): DCE was affiliated with the University of Delhi for many years, which allowed it to award degrees under the University's umbrella. It remained a prominent engineering college in India.
  • Elevation to University Status (2009): In 2009, Delhi College of Engineering (DCE) was granted university status by the Government of Delhi, and it became known as Delhi Technological University (DTU). This change in status allowed DTU to independently award its own degrees.
  • Expansion of Academic Programs: With its new status as a university, DTU expanded its academic programs to include a broader range of engineering disciplines, management studies, and research opportunities. It became one of the leading technical universities in India.
  • Research and Innovation: DTU has been at the forefront of research and innovation in various fields of engineering and technology. It has a strong emphasis on research and collaboration with industries and other academic institutions.
  • Campus and Infrastructure Development: Over the years, DTU has seen significant developments in terms of its campus and infrastructure. New academic buildings, state-of-the-art laboratories, and modern facilities have been added to provide a conducive environment for learning and research.
  • Leadership in Technical Education: DTU is known for its commitment to academic excellence and has produced numerous successful engineers, technologists, and entrepreneurs who have made significant contributions to the industry and society.
Today, Delhi Technological University continues to uphold its legacy as a premier technical institution in India, offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs, and contributing to research and innovation in the field of engineering and technology. It plays a vital role in shaping the future of engineering and technology education in the region.
